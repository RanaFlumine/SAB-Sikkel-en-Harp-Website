Welcome to S.A.B. Sikkel & Harp!

De Socialistische Avonturierders Bond, Sikkel & Harp, is a tabletop role-playing game (TTRPG) society based in Nijmegen. We organize and run role-playing games for everyone who loves adventure, storytelling, and collaboration. Our community is built on inclusivity, solidarity, and, of course, lots of dice rolling! Whether you're an experienced player or just starting out, you're welcome to join us for exciting one-shots and epic campaigns.
We run games in either Dutch or English, depending on your preference.
